Research Areas

Scientific Machine Learning (sciML)

Uncertainty Quantification & Optimal Experimental Design

  • Kuramoto Oscillator Synchronization

    Machine learning-driven robust synchronization of uncertain dynamical systems with optimal control strategies for real-time applications.

  • Neural Message Passing Framework

    Novel framework for quantifying experimental uncertainty while determining optimal designs under resource constraints.

Innovative decision-making algorithms for large-scale virtual screening processes, automating data analysis to accelerate breakthroughs in drug discovery and materials science. Extensions into reinforcement learning for policy optimization.
